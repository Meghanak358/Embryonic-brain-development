Stages of Embryonic Development

  • 1. Fertilization: The sperm and egg combine to form a zygote.
  • 2. Cleavage: The zygote undergoes rapid cell division to form a blastula.
  • 3. Blastocyst Formation: Cells form a hollow ball, which later implants in the uterine wall.
  • 4. Gastrulation: The embryo forms three germ layers: ectoderm, mesoderm, and endoderm.
  • 5. Neural Development: The ectoderm forms the neural tube, the precursor to the brain and spinal cord.
  • 6. Organogenesis: Major organs, including the brain, start forming.
